Our Story

Founded in 2010, Irish Coffee House began as a small corner shop with a big dream: to create a space where coffee isn't just served, but celebrated with the warmth and hospitality of Ireland. Our founders, Siobhan and Patrick, combined their passion for quality beans and traditional brewing methods to create something truly special.

Today, we source our beans directly from small-scale farmers across the globe, ensuring ethical practices and exceptional quality. Every cup is crafted with care, every pastry baked fresh daily, and every customer welcomed like family.

What started as a cozy little shop has grown into a beloved community gathering place. We've expanded our menu to include authentic Irish breakfast offerings and an array of locally sourced treats, but we've never lost sight of our original mission: to serve exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming space.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond our products to our practices. We're proud to say that our coffee house operates with sustainable methods, from our compostable packaging to our energy-efficient equipment. We believe that caring for our planet is just as important as caring for our customers.

When you visit Irish Coffee House, you're not just a customer—you're part of our extended family. We know our regulars by name and their usual orders by heart. We celebrate birthdays, provide comfort during difficult times, and offer a listening ear when needed. It's this sense of community that truly sets us apart.
